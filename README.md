## ✨ What Does CineBox Offer?

**🎞️ Animated Cinematic Hero Banner**
The moment you open the site, you're greeted by a full cinematic hero section — featured films rotate automatically with smooth fade transitions, high-quality backdrop images, and professional lighting effects that make you feel like you're browsing Netflix.

**🔍 Instant Smart Search**
Start typing and autocomplete suggestions appear immediately — complete with movie posters, ratings, and release years — no button press needed.

**🎭 Browse by Genre**
A full sidebar with every movie genre available — Action, Comedy, Horror, Sci-Fi, Anime, and more — each with its own distinct icon for a clean, intuitive navigation experience.

**❤️ Favorites List**
Save any movie to your favorites with a single tap and come back to it anytime. Everything is saved automatically to your device with no account required.

**⏱️ Continue Watching**
CineBox remembers exactly where you left off and surfaces your recent titles in a dedicated "Continue Watching" row every time you open the app.

**📜 Watch History**
A complete log of every movie you've watched, with the ability to clear your history entirely whenever you want — full control, always.

**📺 Server Switcher**
Switch between two streaming servers in one click to get the best quality and fastest load time available at any moment.

**🛡️ Multi-Layer Content Filter**
A smart protection system running silently in the background — filtering out inappropriate content by title, language, genre, vote count, and keyword. Users can also personally hide any card they don't want to see.

**⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts**
Full player control via keyboard — pause, fullscreen, mute, seek forward and backward — all without touching your mouse.

**📱 Fully Responsive Design**
On mobile, the sidebar transforms into smooth horizontal genre pills, cards adapt to any screen size, and the player goes fully fullscreen — no compromises on any device.

## 🎮 Player Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| `Space` | Pause / Resume |
| `F` | Toggle Fullscreen |
| `Esc` | Close Player |
| `←` `→` | Seek Backward / Forward |
| `M` | Mute / Unmute |
